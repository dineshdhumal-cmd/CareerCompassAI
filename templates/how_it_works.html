How Career Compass AI Works

A simple 4-step process to help students discover alternative pathways when their original plans face obstacles.

📝 Step 1 — Describe Your Situation

Share your challenge in natural language.

Examples:

  • Low NEET score
  • Placement rejection
  • Career confusion
  • Academic setback
  • Competitive exam failure

🤖 Step 2 — AI Analysis

Gemini AI analyzes your situation and identifies:

  • Your goal
  • Your original route
  • Current challenges
  • Route feasibility

🧭 Step 3 — Route Recalculation

Career Compass AI generates multiple alternative pathways that can still help you reach your long-term goal.

Instead of focusing on what failed, the system focuses on what is still possible.

💡 Step 4 — Perspective & Guidance

Students receive:

  • Personalized perspective
  • Practical next steps
  • Career guidance
  • Motivation and reassurance

🚀 The Goal

One exam, one rejection, or one setback should never define a student's future.

Career Compass AI helps students recalculate routes and move forward.
